My Story

My name is Mary and I am a very curious and empathetic UX/UI Designer with a passion for art, photography and digital innovation.

I have a background in conservation and restoration of artworks and project management and today I work in the digital industry as User Experience and Interface Designer. During my professional and student life, my attraction for the digital world brought me to e more and more interested in the experience of users related to a digital product and the aility to design not only the interface of it but the experience itself.

Design Process

If you would ever ask me, why do I like to be a UX designer I would reply because I want to be creative, for sure,but this is not the only reason. One of the most fashinating thing that I like of UX design is goign through a user-centered design process., being always ready to put my self in the users’ shoes and change my ideas and my designs when necessary. The goal is to create designs for the users, namely for people, and not for my self.How do I do this? User-centred design process
